Sophia Anastasakis

I fell in love with writing when I was five years old after my kindergarten teacher gifted me a tiny, red marbled notebook. I spent hours bringing my imagination to life with a pen and paper, filling dozens of notebooks over the years.

I followed my passion for storytelling into college, where I am studying digital journalism at Syracuse University. Every day, I have the opportunity to develop a new project, story, or skill. I’ve been able to learn about my community, educate audiences, uncover injustices, and be a part of various campus organizations – including fashion magazines and radio shows. I even had the opportunity to empower young journalists at the School of the New York Times.

I am an enthusiastic learner, strategic thinker, and soon-to-be graduate from Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Communications. My passion for storytelling fuels my academic and professional journey in public relations, journalism, and digital marketing. Upon my graduation, I aim to forge new paths in the industry, leveraging innovation to inspire change and foster meaningful connections.

Outside of the classroom and office, you can find me trying new recipes and listening to my favorite podcasts — a testament to my commitment to continuous learning.
