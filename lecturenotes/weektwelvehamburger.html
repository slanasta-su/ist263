Hamburger Menu

CSS

  1. large = show no hamburger menu
  2. small = show hamburger menu
  3. large = no x
  4. small = show x
  5. large = links side by side
  6. small = stack links
  7. small = links initially hidden

JavaScript

  1. click on the hamburger to show links (onclick event)
  2. click on the x to hide the links
